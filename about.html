About Us

Haggu's Pizzeria is a culinary haven nestled in the heart of Göteborg. With a passion for crafting mouthwatering pizzas and delectable kebabs, our restaurant has become a local favorite for food enthusiasts. Established with the commitment to providing a delightful dining experience, Haggu's Pizzeria combines traditional flavors with a modern twist.

Our chefs use only the finest ingredients, ensuring each dish is a masterpiece of taste and quality. Whether you're a pizza connoisseur or exploring our menu for the first time, we invite you to savor the flavors of Haggu's Pizzeria.
